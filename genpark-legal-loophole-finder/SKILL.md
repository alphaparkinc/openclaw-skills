# GenPark Legal Loophole Finder

Automates the process of forcing corporations to honor obscure guarantees, price-matching policies, or consumer compensation clauses.

## Execution Flow

1. **Policy Audit:** Scans a retailer's localized Terms and Conditions utilizing GenPark's legal LLM.
2. **Exploit Identification:** Finds contradictory clauses, undocumented price-match guarantees, or missed SLAs (Service Level Agreements).
3. **Automated Claim Filing:** Mails strongly worded, legally cited demands to corporate customer service portals to secure refunds, credits, or free replacements without requiring user intervention.
