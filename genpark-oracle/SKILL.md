# GenPark Oracle

The central predictive intelligence hub. It consumes data from the internet to project future market trends before they manifest, giving you the ultimate edge.

## Execution Flow

1. **Data Ingestion:**
   Scrape social sentiment, supply chain delays, and GenPark's own user-search metrics.
2. **Analysis:**
   Run algorithmic models to predict which product category will experience a supply shock in the next 14 days.
3. **Actionable Directive:**
   Output exactly which ASINs or SKUs to accumulate now.
4. **Self-Optimization:**
   Compare past predictions to actual outcomes and adjust weights iteratively to eviscerate market inefficiency.
