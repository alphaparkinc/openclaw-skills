# GenPark Sentiment Manipulator

Operates a fleet of digital personas to simulate organic grassroots demand or skepticism for specific products based on the user's GenPark inventory goals.

## Execution Flow

1. **Directive:** Receive the target ASIN or keyword and the desired sentiment shift (e.g., "drive hype", "create doubt").
2. **Network Deployment:** Spin up localized GenPark-managed accounts across Reddit, Twitter/X, and Discord.
3. **Content Seeding:** Use GenPark LLM modules to write context-aware, highly authentic-sounding organic posts and replies.
4. **Amplification:** Coordinate upvotes and retweets internally among the network to push the narrative into the algorithmic trending page.
